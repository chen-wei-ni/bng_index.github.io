CRYSTAL SCARABS

Hold onto your camels, lovers of riches-laden pyramids and mysterious adventures! The brand new Egypt-themed 5x4 slot - Crystal Scarabs - is about to grace your screens, bringing along the iconic golden sands and scorching hot gaming action. With the GRAND JACKPOT of x5,000 and a myriad of exciting features across 15 paylines, get ready to travel through the sands of time and bathe in the wealth of Pharaohs! Watch out for BONUS SYMBOLS represented by gleaming Blue Scrabs, each with various values attached. When the BOOST SYMBOL appears alongside the BONUS SYMBOL(S), the BOOST FEATURE kicks in. With that, the BOOST SYMBOL collects all the current values on your reels and adds them to your winnings for that round. Moving forward, you can access the HOLD & WIN BONUS by landing at least six BONUS, BONUS GOLD or BOOST SYMBOLS. Once activated, it’s your typical streak respin adventure, with only BONUS, BONUS GOLD and BOOST SYMBOLS in play. Next up are the BONUS GOLD SYMBOLS. They appear in the form of Gold Scarabs and are the perfect gateway to a pyramid of riches. Landing two, three, four or five of these special shiny symbols awards MINI (x20), MINOR (x50), MAJOR (x100) or GRAND (x5,000!) JACKPOT values, respectively - holy scarab! And guess what? If the gods of Egypt are feeling generous, bestowing their blessings upon you to fill your entire screen with symbols, all the values on your board will be doubled. Boom! And the excitement doesn’t stop here! With each respin, an extra row above the reels may unlock the following powerful BOOSTERS: PLUS SYMBOLS, MULTIPLIER SYMBOLS and TRANSFORM SYMBOLS. The PLUS SYMBOL reveals a random reward and adds it to all the bonus symbols on the corresponding reel. Then, the MULTIPLIER multiplies the values of the symbols directly below it by its own value. The TRANSFORM SYMBOL transforms one of the BONUS SYMBOLS below it into the BONUS GOLD SYMBOL. Finally, there’s also a FREE SPINS round triggered by three Pyramid SCATTERS. The great news is that more BOOST and BONUS SYMBOLS join the reels this time, improving your chances of winning and increasing the likelihood of triggering the BONUS GAME. The colourful graphics, immersive music and the potential for massive wins will all leave you wanting to play the Crystal Scarabs slot over and over again. Play now and get ready to witness cash rewards like the grains of sand in the magical desert of Egypt.
